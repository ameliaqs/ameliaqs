UCLA DB Gym Doc :)

Log your gym and perg/erg attendance on your personal gym spreadsheet (linked below). Check the box and insert a note of what workout you did.

Vets: Alongside other factors, we will evaluate overall attendance using this doc when rostering for Blue Boat. Every spot is earned, so put in that effort!

Newbies: You now have the ability to race on Blue Boat if we think you deserve the spot. This doc has a lot of resources if you do not have experience in the gym or need some help with technique or training regimen. Don't be afraid to reach out to vets for advice! Record your attendance in the gym so the coaches can see how hard you work.

  • On your personal gym doc, check the box and write a description of what you did as a note for gym and perg/erg sessions. Full attendance = 2 gym sessions + 2 perg/erg sessions per week
  • I recommend aiming for at least two sessions per week but not more than five
  • Overall gym and practice attendance will be displayed on a leaderboard
  • Land and boat attendance will be recorded by the coaches and will automatically sync to your personal sheet
  • If you cannot attend land, let Amelia know and see the "Land Makeups" page for more instructions.

Reminders

  • Prioritize injury prevention, eating properly, and sufficient sleep for recovery first before worrying about exercising
  • Make sure to do a proper warm up and cooldown! See Warm Up and Cool Down pages for more details
  • If you are feeling injured in any way, let Amelia know or visit the PT room in Wooden (1282D)
  • Logs don't need to be strictly gym (i.e. dance practice, exercise from other clubs, etc.), but make sure you log and aim for two times a week!
